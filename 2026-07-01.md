### 1. BMW Group deploys Figure 03 humanoid after tests with previous version
**宝马集团在测试上一代后部署Figure 03人形机器人**
- Source: The Robot Report  |  Date: Mon, 29 Jun 2026
- BMW Group has deployed Figure 03 humanoid robots after successful tests with Figure 02, which supported production of over 30,000 BMW X3 vehicles in 11 months. This marks a major step in humanoid adoption in automotive assembly.
- 宝马集团在成功测试Figure 02后部署了Figure 03人形机器人。Figure 02曾在11个月内支持生产超过3万辆宝马X3，这标志着人形机器人在汽车装配领域的重大进展。
- Why read: Automotive is a key vertical for cobots; humanoid deployments signal growing acceptance of flexible automation in vehicle assembly.
- 推荐理由：汽车是协作机器人的关键垂直领域；人形机器人的部署表明车辆装配对柔性自动化的接受度正在提高。
- 🔗 https://www.therobotreport.com/bmw-group-deploys-figure-03-humanoid-after-tests-previous-version/

### 3. Sonair ADAR One 3D ultrasonic sensor is now safety-certified
**Sonair ADAR One 3D超声波传感器获得安全认证**
- Source: The Robot Report  |  Date: Tue, 30 Jun 2026
- Sonair's ADAR One 3D ultrasonic sensor gained safety certification per IEC 61496 for human detection. This enables safer human-robot collaboration without compromising sensing reliability.
- Sonair的ADAR One 3D超声波传感器获得IEC 61496人体检测安全认证。这使人在不牺牲传感可靠性的前提下实现更安全的人机协作。
- Why read: Safety certification directly impacts cobot deployment speed and cost—key for sales in SMEs seeking easy integration.
- 推荐理由：安全认证直接影响协作机器人的部署速度和成本——这对寻求简易集成中小企业销售至关重要。
- 🔗 https://www.therobotreport.com/sonair-3d-ultrasonic-sensor-is-now-safety-certified/
