### 1. Hirebotics offers no-code, explosion-proof cobot for painting
**Hirebotics推出免编程防爆协作机器人用于喷涂**
- Source: The Robot Report  |  Date: 2026-06-25
- Hirebotics launched a no-code, explosion-proof cobot designed specifically for painting applications. It supports stationary painting within booths, automating spraying operations without requiring programming expertise.
- Hirebotics推出了一款免编程、防爆的协作机器人，专为喷涂应用设计。它可在喷涂房内执行固定位置喷涂，无需编程技能即可实现自动化。
- Why read: This showcases an emerging vertical for cobots—painting—where safety and ease of deployment are critical. For Huayan, it signals a growing demand for specialized, safe cobots in surface finishing.
- 推荐理由：这展示了协作机器人的新兴垂直领域——喷涂，安全性和易部署性至关重要。对华沿而言，它预示着表面处理领域对专用、安全协作机器人的需求增长。
- 🔗 https://www.therobotreport.com/hirebotics-offers-no-code-explosion-proof-cobot-for-painting/

### 2. Robust.AI chooses Aptiv PULSE sensor for Gen 3 Carter mobile robot
**Robust.AI为第三代Carter移动机器人选用Aptiv PULSE传感器**
- Source: The Robot Report  |  Date: 2026-06-25
- Robust.AI selected Aptiv's PULSE sensor, which fuses radar and vision data with AI, to enable safer human-robot interaction for its Gen 3 Carter mobile robot.
- Robust.AI为其第三代Carter移动机器人选用了Aptiv的PULSE传感器，该传感器通过AI融合雷达和视觉数据，实现更安全的人机交互。
- Why read: Sensor fusion for safe cobot navigation is a growing trend relevant to collaborative robots. For Huayan, understanding perception technologies can inform future product development and positioning.
- 推荐理由：用于安全协作机器人导航的传感器融合是一个增长趋势，与协作机器人相关。对华沿而言，了解感知技术可指导未来产品开发和定位。
- 🔗 https://www.therobotreport.com/robust-ai-chooses-aptiv-pulse-sensor-for-gen-3-carter-mobile-robot/

### 3. General Intuition’s $2.3B bet that video games can train AI agents for the real world
**General Intuition斥资23亿美元押注电子游戏可训练AI智能体用于现实世界**
- Source: TechCrunch  |  Date: 2026-06-25
- General Intuition raised $320 million to scale AI trained on millions of hours of gameplay, aiming to achieve human-like intuition for real-world agent tasks.
- General Intuition筹集了3.2亿美元，用于扩展基于数百万小时游戏训练的人工智能，旨在为现实世界的智能体任务实现类似人类的直觉。
- Why read: Sim-to-real AI training could dramatically reduce robot programming effort. For cobot sales, this means faster deployment and lower costs for complex tasks.
- 推荐理由：从仿真到现实的AI训练可大幅减少机器人编程工作量。对于协作机器人销售，这意味着复杂任务的部署更快、成本更低。
